About Us

About Newark AI Community

We're a nonprofit community organization dedicated to making AI education accessible to every Newark resident—regardless of technical background or experience level.

Our Mission

Artificial Intelligence is transforming how we work, learn, and live. But too often, conversations about AI are filled with jargon, hype, or fear-mongering. We believe Newark residents deserve better.

Our mission is to provide honest, accessible AI education that helps people understand what AI really is, how it can help them, and what they need to know to use it safely and effectively.

What We Do

  • Free Town Halls: Regular in-person and online sessions where we discuss AI topics in plain language
  • Training Sessions: Hands-on workshops teaching practical AI skills for everyday life
  • Content Hub: Blog posts and videos breaking down AI concepts without the technical jargon
  • Discord Community: An ongoing space for questions, discussions, and peer support
  • Community Partnerships: Collaboration with local organizations, libraries, and schools

Our Approach

We believe in:

  • Plain Language: No jargon, no assumptions about technical knowledge
  • Reality-Based Education: Honest conversations about what AI can and can't do
  • Community-Centered: Content and sessions shaped by community needs and questions
  • Hands-On Learning: Practical demonstrations and exercises you can apply immediately
  • Inclusive Access: Free events, multiple formats, and support in multiple languages

Who We Are

Newark AI Community is a partnership between NJIT students, local educators, community organizers, and Newark residents. Our volunteer instructors include:

  • NJIT computer science and AI students
  • Local teachers and librarians
  • Small business owners using AI in their work
  • Community members who've learned AI and want to help others

Our Values

Accessibility

Everyone deserves access to AI education, regardless of background or experience.

Honesty

We tell the truth about AI—both its possibilities and its limitations.

Community

Learning together is more effective and more enjoyable than learning alone.

Empowerment

Knowledge gives people power to make informed decisions about technology.
