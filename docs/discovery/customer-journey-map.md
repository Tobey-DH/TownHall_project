## Key Journey Insights

### Critical Moments of Truth:
1. **First Impression** - Website/flyer must be welcoming and clear
2. **Registration** - Must be simple and accessible
3. **First 10 Minutes** - Welcome and atmosphere sets the tone
4. **First "Aha!" Moment** - When they realize they CAN understand AI
5. **First Application** - When they use AI knowledge in real life
6. **First Giving Back** - When they help another person learn

### Emotional Journey Arc:
```
Intimidated â†’ Curious â†’ Cautiously Optimistic â†’ Excited â†’ 
Confident â†’ Empowered â†’ Advocate â†’ Leader
```

### Success Metrics per Stage:
- **Awareness**: Reach, impressions, inquiries
- **Consideration**: Website visits, email signups, social follows
- **Registration**: Number of registrations, diversity of participants
- **Attendance**: Show-up rate, engagement level
- **Post-Event**: Survey scores, return rate, referrals
- **Engagement**: Active community members, project completions
- **Impact**: Jobs obtained, businesses improved, community projects launched

### Primary User Needs Throughout Journey:
1. **Clarity** - Simple language, clear expectations
2. **Accessibility** - Multiple formats, languages, locations
3. **Relevance** - Newark-specific examples and applications
4. **Community** - Peer support and networking
5. **Progression** - Clear path from beginner to advanced
6. **Impact** - Real-world application and results
