# Goals

## Vision Statement
To make Newark a leading community in AI literacy and empowerment, where every resident understands how AI is rapidly transforming society and has the knowledge, skills, and resources to participate in and benefit from this transformation.

---

## Primary Goals

### 1. Raise Awareness About AI's Rapid Societal Impact üåç

**Objective:** Help Newark residents understand how AI is transforming every aspect of society‚Äîwork, education, healthcare, communication, and daily life.

**Why It Matters:**
AI is changing the world faster than most people realize. Newark residents need to understand:
- How AI is already affecting their jobs and industries
- The opportunities and risks AI presents
- Why they need to engage with AI now, not later
- How to navigate an AI-driven future

**Key Results:**

- 10,000+ Newark residents reached with AI awareness content in Year 1

- Multilingual awareness campaigns

### 2. Provide Accessible, Beginner-Friendly AI Education üìö

**Objective:** Eliminate barriers to AI learning by offering free, jargon-free education that meets Newark residents where they are.

**Why It Matters:**
Most AI education is expensive, technical, and not designed for everyday people. Newark deserves:
- Education that doesn't require a tech background
- Free programs that don't create financial barriers
- Learning in multiple languages and formats
- Content relevant to real community needs

**Key Results:**
- 1,000+ residents complete beginner AI workshops in Year 1
- 90% of participants report feeling "confident" or "very confident" in basic AI concepts
- Workshops available in English, Spanish, and Portuguese
- 100% of workshops are free
- Both in-person and virtual options for every topic
- 80%+ satisfaction rate from participants

**Strategies:**
- "AI 101" workshops with no prerequisites
- Hands-on learning with user-friendly tools
- Small group sizes (15-20 people) for personalized attention
- Multilingual instructors and materials
- Multiple locations across Newark neighborhoods
- Recorded sessions for flexible learning
- No technical jargon without explanation
- "Bring a friend" buddy system for support
