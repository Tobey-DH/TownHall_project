# Personas

## Overview
These personas represent the diverse residents of Newark who would benefit from AI education and community resources through the Newark AI Town Hall. Each persona reflects real community needs, digital literacy levels, and motivations for engaging with AI learning.

---

## Persona 1: Maria Rodriguez - The Curious Beginner

**Demographics:**
- Age: 34
- Occupation: Administrative Assistant at a local non-profit
- Education: Associate's degree
- Location: Ironbound neighborhood, Newark
- Tech proficiency: Basic (comfortable with social media, email, Microsoft Office)

**Background:**
Maria has lived in Newark for 15 years and works at a community organization. She's heard a lot about AI in the news and from her teenage daughter, but feels intimidated by the technical jargon. She wants to understand AI to stay relevant at work and help her daughter navigate technology safely.

**Goals:**
- Learn what AI actually is in simple terms
- Understand how AI affects her daily life and job
- Gain confidence to discuss technology with her children
- Explore if AI skills could advance her career

**Pain Points:**
- Feels overwhelmed by technical language
- Worried about being "too old" or "not tech-savvy enough"
- Limited time due to work and family responsibilities
- Concerned about the cost of courses

**Motivations:**
- Career advancement
- Being a better parent and role model
- Personal growth and staying current
- Community connection

**How Newark AI Town Hall Helps:**
- Free, beginner-friendly workshops in her neighborhood
- Evening and weekend sessions that fit her schedule
- Spanish language support
- Hands-on learning with real-world examples
- Supportive community environment

## Persona 2: James Thompson - The Career Transitioner

**Demographics:**
- Age: 42
- Occupation: Retail manager (looking to change careers)
- Education: High school diploma, some college
- Location: West Ward, Newark
- Tech proficiency: Intermediate (uses smartphones, basic computer skills)

**Background:**
James has worked in retail for 20 years but sees the industry changing. He's heard that AI and tech jobs offer better pay and stability. He wants to transition into a tech-related field but doesn't know where to start or if it's even possible without a four-year degree.

**Goals:**
- Gain practical AI skills for job opportunities
- Network with people in the tech industry
- Learn about career paths that don't require a traditional tech degree
- Build a portfolio or certifications

**Pain Points:**
- Lacks formal tech education or background
- Financial constraints (supporting family)
- Unsure which AI skills are most valuable
- Intimidated by the tech industry culture
- Limited professional network in tech

**Motivations:**
- Better income for his family
- Job security in a growing field
- Personal fulfillment and new challenges
- Being part of Newark's economic growth

**How Newark AI Town Hall Helps:**
- Career-focused workshops and skill-building
- Mentorship from local tech professionals
- Job placement resources and partnerships
- Free certification programs
- Networking events with Newark employers
- Success stories from similar career changers

## Persona 3: Destiny Williams - The Student Innovator

**Demographics:**
- Age: 19
- Occupation: College student (studying Business at Rutgers-Newark)
- Education: Currently pursuing Bachelor's degree
- Location: University Heights, Newark
- Tech proficiency: High (social media native, comfortable with apps and tools)

**Background:**
Destiny grew up in Newark and is the first in her family to attend college. She's interested in entrepreneurship and has heard that AI is transforming business. She wants to learn how to use AI tools to start her own business or bring innovation to Newark's small business community.

**Goals:**
- Learn AI tools that can help with business (marketing, analytics, automation)
- Build projects for her portfolio
- Connect with other young innovators in Newark
- Understand AI ethics and responsible use
- Find internship or job opportunities

**Pain Points:**
- College courses are theoretical; wants practical skills
- Expensive online courses and certifications
- Doesn't see enough Black women in AI spaces
- Wants to give back to Newark but unsure how

**Motivations:**
- Career preparation and competitive advantage
- Community impact and representation
- Creative expression through technology
- Building wealth for her family

**How Newark AI Town Hall Helps:**
- Hands-on workshops with real AI tools
- Student-focused project groups
- Mentorship from diverse AI professionals
- Free access to resources and tools
- Community projects that serve Newark
- Networking with local entrepreneurs and startups

## Persona 4: Robert Chen - The Small Business Owner

**Demographics:**
- Age: 51
- Occupation: Owner of a family restaurant in the Ironbound
- Education: Bachelor's degree (Taiwan)
- Location: Ironbound neighborhood, Newark
- Tech proficiency: Basic to Intermediate (uses POS systems, social media for business)

**Background:**
Robert runs a successful restaurant that's been in his family for 25 years. He's seeing competitors use technology in new ways and wants to keep up. He's curious about AI for customer service, inventory management, and marketing but doesn't have time to wade through complex tutorials.

**Goals:**
- Learn specific AI tools for restaurant operations
- Improve customer experience and retention
- Streamline business operations
- Stay competitive with chain restaurants

**Pain Points:**
- Very limited time (works 60+ hours/week)
- Budget constraints for business technology
- Language barriers (more comfortable in Mandarin)
- Skeptical about ROI of new technology
- Needs immediate, practical applications

**Motivations:**
- Business growth and sustainability
- Passing a thriving business to his children
- Serving the Newark community better
- Reducing operational stress

**How Newark AI Town Hall Helps:**
- Short, focused workshops on specific business use cases
- Multilingual support and materials
- Cost-benefit analysis and ROI guidance
- Local business owner peer network
- Office hours for one-on-one consultation
- Case studies from Newark businesses

## Persona 5: Patricia Johnson - The Community Leader

**Demographics:**
- Age: 58
- Occupation: Director at a Newark community center
- Education: Master's degree in Social Work
- Location: South Ward, Newark
- Tech proficiency: Intermediate (uses computers for work, video calls, basic software)

**Background:**
Patricia has dedicated her career to serving Newark residents, particularly youth and seniors. She sees AI as both an opportunity and a potential threat to her community. She wants to understand AI to advocate for her community's needs and ensure equitable access to AI education and benefits.

**Goals:**
- Understand AI's impact on Newark communities
- Bring AI literacy programs to her community center
- Advocate for ethical AI and data privacy
- Connect residents to AI resources
- Address digital divide issues

**Pain Points:**
- Concerned about AI bias and discrimination
- Worried about job displacement in the community
- Sees gaps in tech access among seniors and low-income families
- Wants to ensure AI benefits everyone, not just the privileged
- Limited budget for programs

**Motivations:**
- Community advocacy and equity
- Empowering underserved populations
- Fighting digital divide
- Ensuring Newark residents aren't left behind

**How Newark AI Town Hall Helps:**
- Partnership opportunities for community organizations
- Train-the-trainer programs
- Resources she can share with her community
- Focus on AI ethics and equity
- Senior-friendly and accessibility-focused content
- Grant and funding information for programs

## Persona 6: Marcus Davis - The Tech-Curious Teen

**Demographics:**
- Age: 16
- Occupation: High school student at Newark Tech
- Education: Currently in high school
- Location: Central Ward, Newark
- Tech proficiency: High (gaming, coding basics, social media)

Marcus is interested in technology and coding but his high school's resources are limited. He follows AI YouTubers and wants to learn how to build AI projects. He's looking for free resources, mentorship, and a community of peers who share his interests.

**Goals:**
- Build AI projects (chatbots, games, apps)
- Prepare for college computer science programs
- Get internship or summer opportunities
- Connect with mentors in tech
- Learn skills not taught in school

**Pain Points:**
- Limited resources at school
- Can't afford expensive courses or software
- Doesn't know anyone personally who works in AI
- Uncertain about college affordability
- Wants hands-on experience, not just theory

**Motivations:**
- Career preparation
- Creative expression
- Peer community and belonging
- Making his family proud
- Giving back to Newark

**How Newark AI Town Hall Helps:**
- Youth-focused coding and AI workshops
- Hackathons and project competitions
- Mentorship from Newark tech professionals
- College preparation and scholarship info
- Free tools and software access
- Summer programs and internships
- Teen leadership opportunities

## Summary

These six personas represent the diverse Newark community the AI Town Hall serves:

1. **Maria** - Adult learners seeking basic AI literacy
2. **James** - Career changers looking for economic opportunity
3. **Destiny** - College students preparing for the future
4. **Robert** - Business owners seeking practical applications
5. **Patricia** - Community leaders ensuring equitable access
6. **Marcus** - Youth building skills and exploring careers

Each has unique needs, constraints, and motivations, but all share a desire to engage with AI in ways that are accessible, practical, and beneficial to themselves and the Newark community.
