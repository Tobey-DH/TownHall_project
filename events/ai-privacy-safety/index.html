AI Privacy & Safety Town Hall

Date & Time

February 20, 2026

11:00 PM

Location

NJIT Campus Center, Room 202, Newark, NJ 07102

Capacity: 100 attendees

Learn how to protect yourself and your family when using AI tools. Understand data privacy, bias in AI, and how to use AI safely and responsibly.

AI is powerful, but it's important to understand the privacy and safety considerations. Join us for an honest conversation about using AI responsibly.

Topics We'll Cover

Privacy & Data: What information do AI tools collect? How is your data used and shared? Settings to protect your privacy. What to NEVER share with AI.

Bias & Fairness: How AI can inherit human biases. Real examples of AI bias affecting communities. What companies are doing to fix it. How to recognize and report biased AI.

Safe Usage: Teaching children about AI safety. Protecting your business data. Verifying AI-generated information. Recognizing AI scams and deepfakes.

Expert Panel

Hear from a privacy lawyer explaining your rights, AI researcher from NJIT, community advocate discussing AI equity, and cybersecurity expert on data protection.

What You'll Learn

  • ✓ Understand what data AI companies collect and how to protect your privacy
  • ✓ Recognize bias in AI and how it might affect you
  • ✓ Learn safe practices for using AI tools with personal information
  • ✓ Know your rights regarding AI and data collection

Ready to Join Us?

Registration is free and open to all Newark residents.
