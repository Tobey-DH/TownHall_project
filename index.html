Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to bring a laptop?

For most events: Optional but recommended. Intro sessions are lecture-style (no laptop needed). Hands-on workshops require a laptop. Check each event descriptionâ€”we'll always tell you what to bring.

How technical is this really?

Not technical at all. If you can use Google or email, you can follow our workshops. We explain everything in plain language. No coding, no math, no jargon.

Can this help with college applications? (Students)

Yes! Our "Build Your First AI Project" workshop gives you a portfolio piece, GitHub repo, and certificateâ€”all great for college apps. Past students have used these projects to get into NJIT, Rutgers, and Stevens.

Are these events really free?

100% free. No hidden costs, no upsells. Funded by NJIT and community partners. We provide materials, refreshments, and certificates at no cost.

What if I can't make it in person?

Many events are online or hybrid. Check the event page for location type. Online events use Zoom (free). In-person events are recorded when possible.

Will I actually save time using AI?

If you implement 2-3 tools, yes. Our workshops focus on practical time-savers: email drafting (save 2 hrs/week), social media posts (save 3 hrs/week), meeting summaries (save 1 hr/week). We'll be honest about what works and what doesn't.
